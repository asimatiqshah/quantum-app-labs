Course Overview

Digital Marketing is the backbone of every modern business's online growth strategy. This comprehensive course equips you with the skills to drive organic traffic through powerful Search Engine Optimization (SEO) techniques, run high-converting Google & Meta ad campaigns, design engaging social media content strategies, and measure performance with precision analytics.

Whether you're a business owner wanting to grow your brand, a freelancer looking to offer in-demand services, or a career switcher entering the marketing field — this program takes you from foundational concepts to advanced execution. You will work on real client-style projects, set up live campaigns, and build a portfolio-ready case study by the end of the program.

What You'll Learn

Master on-page & off-page SEO techniques for Google ranking.
Run profitable Google Ads (PPC) and Meta Ad campaigns.
Build a full content marketing strategy for any niche.
Perform keyword research using Ahrefs, SEMrush & Google tools.
Grow social media accounts organically across all platforms.
Analyze campaigns with Google Analytics 4 & Search Console.
Design high-converting email marketing funnels.
Freelance as a certified digital marketing consultant.
